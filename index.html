Eddie Guerrero

WWE Hall of Famer

Here's a time line of Eddie Guerrero's wrestling career:

  • 1987-He begins his professional wrestling career as Máscara Mágica in the CMLL.
  • 1992-Guerrero begins his professional wrestling career in Japan, where he is known as the second incarnation of Black Tiger.
  • 1997-At Clash of the Champions XXXIV, Guerrero successfully defends his U.S. Heavyweight Title.
  • 2000-On “Raw Is War,” Guerrero makes his WWF debut with The Radicalz, interfering in a match between The New Age Outlaws.
  • 2002-Guerrero makes his World Wrestling All-Stars debut at The Revolution, winning the WWA International Cruiserweight Championship in a Triple Threat Match against champion Juventud Guerrera and Psicosis.
  • 2005-Guerrero defeats Mr. Kennedy on SmackDown! by disqualification, advancing to the SmackDown! Survivor Series.

Eddie Guerrero worked incredibly hard to get to where he was when he passed, all the way from the bottom to the top. And for that, we thank you Eddie. May you continue resting in peace.

--Leva Lies
